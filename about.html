> About Me

I'm a system engineer with a strong background in enterprise IT infrastructure, automation, and cloud technologies. At NMBS/SNCB, the Belgian national railway company, I manage mobile devices using platforms like Microsoft Intune, Samsung Knox, and SureMDM, while also overseeing key cloud services such as Microsoft Exchange and Entra ID. My IT journey began in hands-on support and system administration, and over the years I’ve grown into a senior engineering role, gaining deep expertise in the Microsoft enterprise stack, PowerShell scripting, and modern DevOps practices with tools like Ansible, Terraform, and containerized deployments. I’m also passionate about Linux, immutable infrastructure, and server hardening, with a particular interest in NixOS and Fedora Atomic. Outside of work, I enjoy running a personal homelab where I continually explore and experiment with new technologies to improve automation, performance, and security.
