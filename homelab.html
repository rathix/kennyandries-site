My Homelab: Real-World Infrastructure, Built from the Ground Up

As a System Engineer and IT Consultant, I believe deeply in mastering the technologies I recommend. That's why I've built and maintain a production-grade homelab that mirrors the standards, scalability, and resilience expected in professional environments.

This infrastructure is more than just a playground - it's a self-hosted, continuously evolving platform where I design, deploy, and maintain real services with enterprise-grade principles.

> Core Stack: Proxmox VE Cluster

  • High Availability (HA)
  • Live Migration
  • Centralized Management
  • ZFS-backed storage

This setup allows me to simulate and manage real infrastructure scenarios, including rolling upgrades, service migrations, and performance optimization.

> Resilient Backup Strategy

I've implemented both on-site and remote Proxmox Backup Servers, providing secure, deduplicated, and encrypted backups across my environment.

  • Disaster recovery across physical locations
  • Fast, incremental restores
  • Snapshot-based protection for VMs and containers

> Actively Managed Services

My homelab runs a suite of interconnected, production-grade applications, all self-hosted:

  • Nextcloud - Private file storage, calendar, and contact syncing
  • Jellyfin - Media server for streaming content locally and remotely
  • Vaultwarden - Self-hosted password management (Bitwarden-compatible)
  • Pocket ID - Self-hosted identity provider for SSO across services
  • Home Assistant - Full-stack smart home automation and control
  • Immich - AI-based photo backup and management
  • Servarr Stack - Media acquisition automation (Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr, etc.)

These services are reverse proxied, monitored, and secured as they would be in production, demonstrating my end-to-end understanding of deployment, orchestration, and lifecycle management.

> Network & Security Architecture

  • Reverse proxy with HTTPS and strict access control
  • Identity-based authentication (via my own IdP)
  • Internal network segmentation and firewall policies
  • Site-to-site VPN for remote access and replication
  • Real-time monitoring and alerting

Security is treated as a first-class citizen in every layer of the stack.
