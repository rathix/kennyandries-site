About

System engineer from Belgium. I like building things that work and automating the stuff nobody wants to do twice.

I worked my way up from helpdesk through sysadmin to where I am now, so I know what it's like at every level of the stack. That background shapes how I approach problems. I'd rather build something sustainable than slap a bandaid on it.

I'm the kind of person who automates the tedious stuff. PowerShell, Ansible, and Terraform are my go-to tools. I also spend a lot of time with Linux, containers (Docker, Podman), and immutable systems like NixOS and Fedora Atomic. Outside work I run a homelab where I break things on purpose and figure out better approaches. I do some consulting on the side too.

Technical experience

I work across the full Microsoft stack (Intune, Exchange, Entra ID, Azure) and Linux ecosystems, with deep expertise in enterprise mobility management, zero-touch device provisioning, and infrastructure automation. My day-to-day involves solving operational problems at scale, from deploying Knox Suite across thousands of Samsung devices to managing Android Enterprise and iOS deployments for complex operational environments.

What I do now

At NMBS/SNCB (Belgian National Railways) I manage a fleet of 30,000+ devices for over 20,000 users. That includes smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, Zebra scanners, and rugged hardware for train drivers, train managers, maintenance crews, and security staff. These aren't office workers checking email. When a device fails in the field, operations feel it immediately.

On the MDM side I work with Microsoft Intune, Samsung Knox, and SureMDM. Separately, I also manage Exchange and Entra ID. If you're looking for someone who's dealt with large-scale device management in a mission-critical environment, let's talk.

What drives me

Building systems that scale securely with minimal manual intervention. I believe good infrastructure should be automated, reproducible, and built with security as a foundation, not an afterthought.

Actively deepening my DevOps/DevSecOps skills with Kubernetes, security automation, and observability tooling while helping organizations solve complex IT challenges through consulting.
